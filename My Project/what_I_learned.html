What I Learned

From this project, I found that my friends did not like to buy candles online. They were all internet active with highspeed internet. They all at least purchased product from Amazon and other big websites which were more convenient to them. They all found that the websites were easy to use. The target users of the website probably were not my friends or maybe we had only 4 surveys. If we collected more survey from different group of people, the result could be more valuable and review more hidden information.
