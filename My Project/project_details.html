Project Details

The project is about testing the usability of Yankee Candle and Goose Greek Candle website. The data is captured by 3 surveys including user information, usability of Yankee and Goose Greek candles website from 4 participants who are at least 18, have used internet in the pax six months to a small degree. The surveys are sent and collected from 4 participants by email.
